Impala Park Site Plan

Site Name

ImpalaParkKenya.org

This domain name was chosen because it represents the wildlife park in Kenya and is easy for visitors to identify when searching for information about the park.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as an information hub for visitors to learn about the wildlife, events, and tours offered at Impala Park. It will also allow visitors to book tours and get detailed information about the conservation efforts happening in the park.

Target Audience Scenarios

  • Where can I book a wildlife tour and see available time slots?
  • What species of animals can I expect to see during my visit?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: Forest Green (#228B22) - Used for headings, navigation bar, and buttons.
  • Accent Color: Golden Yellow (#FFD700) - Used for highlights, important information, and borders.
  • Background Color: Off-White (#F5F5F5) - Used for the background of the site to keep a clean and neutral look.

Typography

  • Heading Font: Lora, serif - Used for all headings (H1, H2, H3) to provide an elegant look.
  • Body Font: Open Sans, sans-serif - Used for the body text to ensure readability and clarity.
